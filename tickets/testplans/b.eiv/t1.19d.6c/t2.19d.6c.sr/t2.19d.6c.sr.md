# subprocess-client-2-serialization

Per SRD §SR-10.4 + CSCB SR-0: each Client instance serializes verb
calls through a private chained-Promise queue. Mirrors
`pkg/ts-bun-client/test/client-serialization.test.ts` against the
installed-tarball layout.

## Setup

- `${HOME}=$(mktemp -d)`.
- `bun install file:///work/source/dist/agent-director-*.tgz` into
  `${HOME}/proj`.

## Steps

1. Write/copy a driver that constructs one Client, fires 5 parallel
   `spawn` calls (with distinct `seq=N` labels), awaits `Promise.all`,
   then `list`s and inspects `started_at` ordering by label.
2. Also fires one `version()` call on each of two independent Clients
   in parallel to confirm cross-Client overlap is allowed (SR-3.2).

## Pass criteria

- Driver exits 0.
- The 5 spawned rows are recovered, indexed by `seq=N`, and their
  `started_at` timestamps are monotonically non-decreasing in
  submission order, with at least one strict positive gap (rules out
  "all simultaneous").
- The two-client `version()` Promise.all resolves both promises
  without deadlock.

```bash
set -euo pipefail

echo "subprocess-client-2-serialization ok"
```
